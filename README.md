# 🎯 SPM CEFR English Grade Estimator

A free, student-friendly web app to estimate your English CEFR grade for SPM 2025 — based on individual paper marks and target grade!

---

## 📚 How It Works

Just enter your marks for each paper:

| Paper                    | Max Marks |
|--------------------------|-----------|
| Paper 1 – Reading        | 40        |
| Paper 2 – Writing        | 60        |
| Paper 3 – Speaking       | 24        |
| Paper 4 – Listening      | 30        |

✅ The app will calculate:
- Your **current percentage**
- If you enter a **Target (ETR)**, it will tell you what score you need in remaining papers to reach it!

---

## 🖨️ Features

- 📊 Real-time grade and percentage calculation
- 🎯 "ETR" — *Estimated To Reach* target function
- 🖨️ **Print Report** button for easy printing or submission
- 🔄 **Reset All** button to start over anytime
- 💡 Simple layout, mobile friendly, no login needed
